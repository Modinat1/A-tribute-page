David Bowie

Born on the 8th, January 1947 - Died on the 10th January 2016

David Robert Jones, better known by the stage name David Bowie, was an English singer-songwriter and actor

    SIGNIFICANT HAPPENINGS DURING HIS LIFETIME

  • David Bowie's career was marked to be reinvention and visual presentation, with his music and stagecraft having significant impact on popular music
  • His record sales was estimated to be at over 100 million records worldwide, which made him one of the best-selling muisc artists of all times
  • He was awarded ten platinum and nine gold certification, eleven gold and eight silver and released eleven number-one albums, in the UK
  • He received five platinum and nine certification, in the US
  • He was inducted into the Rock and Roll Hall of Fame in 1996
  • Rolling Stone placed him among its list of 100 Greatest Artist of All Times
  • Following his death 2016, Bowie was dubbed "The Greatest Rock Star Ever" by the Times magazine
